Why there are no comic images on this site

Simple: we don't own them, and respecting Bill Watterson's wishes is kind of the whole ethos around here. He famously turned down millions in merchandising to protect the strip's integrity. The least a fan blog can do is write its own words and send you to the official archive to see the real thing — starting with the very first strip (Nov 18, 1985) and ending with the final one (Dec 31, 1995).
